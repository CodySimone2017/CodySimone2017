Our History

Cody and Simone's bid for SGA president started out when they were annoyed at how many signs there were springing up all over campus like weeds. Like really, thats at least $400 worth of signs. I made these myself on the cheap.

I think we've know each other for like, six months? Oh and we choose our slogan because we aren't on the ballot and want to toss in a reality check to the whole affair.

The Issues

It isn't like SGA platforms are even controversial. We want more bus hours, better services, a sauna in the gym, less tuition. Yea, real hard hitting issues, very divisive. But budgets exist and it's not like SGA is the Florida Senate or the Board of Trustees. I say we focus on two things which we can actually accomplish.

  • Advocate for a more sensible UCF budget.
  • Build a sense of community on campus.

Our REAL Issues
